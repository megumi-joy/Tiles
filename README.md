## 🌟 About The Game

**Tiles** is a mesmerizing, fast-paced puzzle adventure where every move counts. Guide your sphere through a dynamic, ever-changing landscape of colorful tiles, avoiding traps and finding the perfect drop zone. 

With deep physics-based movement, endless procedural levels, and an evolving difficulty curve, **Tiles** offers an endless state of "flow" for players looking for both relaxation and a serious challenge.

### 🔥 Key Features

* **Infinite Procedural Worlds**: No two runs are the same. Experience beautifully shifting color palettes and dynamically generated platforms.
* **Masterful Physics**: "Easy to learn, hard to master" movement mechanics. Feel the weight, momentum, and drag of the ball.
* **AI Autopilot**: Watch the built-in AI solve the hardest paths with perfect, robotic precision.
* **Seamless Cross-Platform Controls**: Play seamlessly on Desktop (Keyboard) or Mobile (Virtual Joystick/Touch).
* **Deep Progression System** *(Coming Soon)*: Unlock new spheres, compete on global leaderboards, and prove your skills in daily challenges!
