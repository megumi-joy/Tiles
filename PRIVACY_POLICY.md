# Privacy Policy

**MagicBallsAdventure**
*Last updated: March 18, 2026*

## Introduction

MagicBallsAdventure ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile puzzle-adventure game developed as an independent project. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user and device data.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

The app does not:
- Require user accounts or registration
- Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Include third-party advertising SDKs
- Make any network requests or internet connections

## Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Leaderboard records** — your best completion times per level, saved as a local JSON file in the app's private storage (`leaderboard.json`). This data never leaves your device.
- **Game settings** — preferences such as volume levels and graphics quality, stored in memory during gameplay.

This data is never transmitted to any server or third party.

## Device Information

The app reads basic device information (operating system name, device model, screen size, and graphics adapter) solely for the purpose of optimizing performance and display settings. This information is used only at runtime and is **never collected, stored persistently, or transmitted**.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected or transmitted, the app is safe for users of all ages.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that could collect user data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
