# Privacy Policy

**Tiles**
*Last updated: March 18, 2026*

## Introduction

**Tiles** ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile puzzle-adventure game. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user and device data, including online features such as analytics, advertisements, and optional cloud saves.

## Data We Collect and Use

As the game evolves, we collect and process data to improve your experience, provide cloud saves, display relevant ads, and monitor game performance.

### 1. Analytics and Crash Reporting
We use third-party services to monitor game performance, understand player behavior, and fix crashes. These services may collect:
- Device information (OS version, device model, screen size)
- App performance data and crash logs
- Anonymous usage data (playtime, levels completed)

*Providers used:* Google Analytics for Firebase, Firebase Crashlytics, Supabase.

### 2. Advertising
We use third-party advertising networks to show ads within the game. These networks may use temporary device identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads and track ad performance.
*Providers used:* AdMob, Google Play Services.

### 3. Account and Cloud Data
We offer optional account registration (Login) to enable Cloud Saves. If you choose to log in:
- We securely store your login credentials through our authentication providers.
- We save your gameplay data, leaderboard records, and settings in the cloud so you can sync them across devices.
*Providers used:* Firebase Authentication, Supabase.

### 4. In-App Purchases (IAP)
The app offers in-app purchases. All transactions are securely processed through trusted third-party billing providers (e.g., Google Play Billing, cryptocurrency payment gateways). We do not process, collect, or store your credit card information or direct billing details on our servers.

## Local Storage

Even if you play entirely offline, the app stores data locally on your device:
- **Leaderboard records** — your best completion times per level.
- **Game settings** — preferences such as volume levels and graphics quality.

## Children's Privacy

The app is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
